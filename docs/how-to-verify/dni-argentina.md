## DNI verification on RENAPER website

DNI (Documento Nacional de Identidad) search option is available for free on the Argentina RENAPER website. RENAPER (Registro Nacional de las Personas) is Argentina's national civil registry and identity document issuing authority website. You can access the dni search [here](https://tramites.renaper.gob.ar/mi_ejemplar/). This search requires you to add the date of birth and the sex for successful dni validation.
