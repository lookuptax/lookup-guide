## VAT number Search on National Revenue Agency website

VAT number search option is available for free in Bulgaria National Revenue Agency (NRA) website under Services option. Go to service option on the NRA website, click on the icon Public registers and lists, click on the option "Provision of a reference for persons registered under the Value Added Tax Act and persons granted permission to apply the VAT cash reporting regime /electronic service with free access/ No. 2657". You will be redirected to the search tool. You can directly go to the VAT number search [here](https://portal.nra.bg/details/report-cash-terminal). This tool allows you to search for a company by its VAT number. You can also search by the identications such as EIK,EGN,LNCH,LN,Sl numbers.

* Basis for VAT registration	
* Date of VAT deregistration

* Date of application based on Art. 151a of VAT	
* Date of deregistration based on Art. 151a of VAT

## Estores Search on National Revenue Agency website

Interestingly Bulgaria's National Revenue Agency also maintains a list of estores with them. The NRA website allows you to search for a company with online store by it name or a few other details. To access this search go to service option on the NRA website, click on the icon Public registers and lists, click on the option "List of electronic stores registered with the National Revenue Agency". You can alternatively visit the search page directly from [here](https://portal.nra.bg/details/online-store-nrareg)

This search option allows you to search by the following options
* Name of the e-store	
* Web address of the e-shop	
* Online Platform Web Address
* Name/pseudonym/identifier under which the goods/services are offered on the platform	
* Type of goods sold
* Sales Person ID
* Name of the person making the sales
